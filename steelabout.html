Committed to Excellence in Product & Service

National Building Group Inc. is a premier metal building supply company committed to providing top-quality customer-engineered metal buildings. These buildings meet design specifications, perform reliably, and look great for decades to come.

At National Building Group Inc., we take pride in being an industry leader in pre-engineered Steel Building Construction. We service agricultural, commercial, industrial and recreational markets. Our buildings are fully customizable — options include mezzanines, heated floors, ventilation, doors, overhangs and more.

NBGI Steel Building Advantages
  • Premium gray primer on main frames for a contemporary look. Optional hot-dip galvanized or red-oxide primer available.
  • Optional Hot-Dip Galvanized components.
  • Rust-resistant galvanized door jambs with extended salt-spray life (ASTM B-117).
  • In-house certified welding inspector — commitment to quality.
  • Roof and wall panels feature long-life finishes with up to 40-year warranties (color dependent).
  • Purlin Bearing Rib (PBR) panels for stronger, weather-tight fit. Optional standing-seam roof.
  • Galvanized purlins, girts and door framing.
  • Anchor bolt and building drawings ready in 7 days.
  • Building delivery in 4–6 weeks.
